Space Group 088 : I 41 / a

(Return link to main list of space groups in standard settings.)

A large number of origin positions is given for this space group simply to demonstrate the possible permutations.

(a). I 41 / a with origin at -4 and -1 at 0,1/4,1/8.
(b). I 41 / a with origin at -1 and -4 at 0,1/4,1/8.
(c). I 41 / a with origin at -1 and -4 at 0,1/4,3/8.
(d). I 41 / a with origin at -1 and -4 at 1/4,0,1/8.
(e). I 41 / a with origin at -1 and -4 at 1/4,0,3/8.
(f). I 41 / a with origin at -4 and -1 at 0,1/4,3/8.
(g). I 41 / a with origin at 4(1) / a and -1 at 0,1/4,0.
(h). I 41 / a with origin at 4(1) / b and -1 at 1/4,0,0.
(i). I 41 / a with origin at 4(3) / a and -1 at 0,1/4,0.
(j). I 41 / a with origin at 4(3) / b and -1 at 1/4,0,0.
