Note on Diagrams for Space Groups P4332, P4132, & I4132

(Return to main list of space groups in standard settings)

Space groups P4332, P4132, and I4132 have all been drawn showing both the lower left quadrant of the unit cell and the complete unit cell. However, due to overcrowding of the symmetry elements, the complete unit cell cannot show all of the threefold screw axes and inclined twofold axes simultaneously. Due to this restriction, some of the inclined twofold axes within the complete unit cell are deliberately omitted for clarity. Their position is readily deduced from other equivalent parallel twofold axes.
